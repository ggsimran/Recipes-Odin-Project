Recipe forCheeseCake

Description

Creamy, rich and absolutely irrestible dessert made with cream cheese, eggs, sugar and baked in a crust.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted melted butter
  • 24 oz softenedcream cheese
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 F (160 C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the cracker crumbs and butter. Put it on a pan and bake it for 10 minutes.
  3. Let it cool, then in a large bowl beat cream cheese, sugar, vanilla extract, add eggs, sour cream and flour until well combined.
  4. Pour the filling over the crust. bake for another 55-60 minutes. Let it cool and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  5. Serve chilled and Enjoyyyy!
