Recipe for Brownie

Description

Fudgy, rich and absolutely irresistible classic dessert made with chocolate, butter, sugar, eggs and flour.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch baking pan.
  • Melt the butter in a large saucepan, stir in sugar, eggs and vanilla extract until well combined.
  • In a separate bowl, flour, cocoa powder, salt and baking powder add dry ingredients to the wet ingredients mixing until just combined.
  • Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread evenly.
  • Bake in preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  • Remove from oven and let cool completely before cutting into squares. Enjoyyyy!
